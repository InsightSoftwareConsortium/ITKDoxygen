Detailed Description

template<typename TInputImage, typename TFeatureImage, typename TOutputImage = LabelMap<StatisticsLabelObject<typename TInputImage::PixelType, TInputImage::ImageDimension>>>
class itk::LabelImageToStatisticsLabelMapFilter< TInputImage, TFeatureImage, TOutputImage >

a convenient class to convert a label image to a label map and valuate the statistics attributes at once

Author
Gaetan Lehmann. Biologie du Developpement et de la Reproduction, INRA de Jouy-en-Josas, France.

This implementation was taken from the Insight Journal paper: https://www.insight-journal.org/browse/publication/176

See also
